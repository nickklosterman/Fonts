
Anke Calligraphic FG

This beautiful handrwiting is from Anke Arnold. You can still find
her signature on braceright + asciitilde. Originally named AnkeCalligraph
it is not on her website (www.anke-art.de) any more, but you may find
it in one or two shareware archives.

I slightly changed some of the letters for a more even look. But 
most of the work was done on spacing to give the font a more flowing
look. Of course the usual international characters were added.

December 2002

Andreas Höfeld
